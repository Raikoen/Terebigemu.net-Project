HEROES


Kairi

A kind, loving friend of Sora and Riku, the one whom both would do anything to find.
Riku

One of Sora's best friends who beat the Darkness and searches for Sora.
Namine

A witch with power over Sora's memories and a friend of Roxas.
Roxas

Organization XIII's figment of Sora's memories, wields the Keyblade.
Sora

Uses the keyblade to destroy Heartless and Nobodies. Still looks for Riku.





















VILLIANS

Xemnas

The last boss, defeated by both Sora and Riku.
Roxas

He isn't evil, but he is somewhat against Sora regardless.
Organization XIII

Probably the main antagonist group Sora has to defeat.
Pete

The very annoying villian that seeks to control all Heartless.
The Twilight Thorn

The first boss-level Nobody in the game, which Roxas defeats.
